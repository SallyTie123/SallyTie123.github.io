What is XMind?

XMIND is a whole new amazing mind mapping and brainstorming software, developed by XMind Ltd.

Why do you need XMind?

In addition to the management elements, the software can capture ideas, clarify thinking, manage complex information, and promote team collaboration for higher productivity.

How do you use XMind?

1.Download and open XMind 8 - don't worry, it's free.

2.Create a central topic for your mind map - one map has only one central topic, with others being branches.

3.Press "Insert" on your keyboard, and you can get a branch attached to the main topic.

4.If you press "Insert" again, then you will create a subtopic of the current topic.

5.To create a main topic or a topic on the same level, press "Enter".

6.For those who love diversity, press the second icon on the right column, and you can change everything about your map from font, color to size.
